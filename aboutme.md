My name is Blaine Weiss. I am a graduate student at the University of Kentucky working in the Sanders Brown Center on Aging. My research interests involve utilizing brain electrophysiology, intravital fluorescence imaging, and custom analytical techniques to investigate physiological characteristics of neurological diseases and therapeutic efficacy.
